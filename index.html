📐 Grammar Lab

What to do

Choose a topic, read the rule, then tap the correct answer. Present Continuous shows what is happening right now (am / is / are + verb-ing). Prepositions of movement show how someone moves from place to place.

Step 1 — choose am / is / are

I → am · he / she / it → is · we / you / they → are

“They are running on the sand.”

Step 2 — add -ing to the verb

run → running, take → taking, swim → swimming

“She is taking a photo right now.”

In, out, through

into = inside · out of = to the outside · through = from one side to the other

“The cat runs into the house.”

Up, down, near, away

over = above · under = below · towards = closer · away from = leaving

“He jumps over the rock.”
